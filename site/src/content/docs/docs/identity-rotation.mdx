Identity rotation (decision ORP-004, SPEC §12) lets a device change its
identity keys over time without tearing down the rendezvous bindings it
already holds.

## Why rotation

Long-lived identity keys are a liability: the longer a key lives, the more
exposure a compromise carries. Rotation lets a device retire an old identity
key and adopt a new one on a schedule or after a suspected compromise.

## How a rotation works
